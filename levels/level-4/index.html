Virtuoso Bow Techniques

⚡ Flying Staccato

A series of staccato notes in ONE bow direction (usually up-bow). The wrist and fingers create each staccato impulse while the arm moves slowly in one direction.

  1. Begin: 4 notes per bow at ♩=60 (up-bow)
  2. Increase to 8, 12, 16 notes
  3. Goal: 16+ rapid notes in one smooth bow
🔥 Ricochet / Jeté

The bow is thrown onto the string at the balance point and bounces multiple times automatically. Unlike spiccato, ricochet is uncontrolled bouncing for specific effects.

  1. Drop the bow from ~2cm at the balance point
  2. It bounces 4–6 times naturally
  3. Control by adjusting stick angle and initial force

✍️ Improvisation Foundations

Improvising over Chord Changes

  1. Major chord = major scale/Ionian
  2. Minor chord = Dorian or Aeolian
  3. Dominant 7th = Mixolydian
  4. Target chord tones on strong beats
  5. Connect with passing tones
